Data protection policy

Thank you for using our website. To protect the security of your personal data, we have developed this data protection policy, which describes how we collect, use, process and protect your personal data.

1. Type of personal data collected

When you use our website, we collect the following personal data via the contact form:

  • First name and surname
  • E-mail address
  • Phone number

2. Purpose of data collection

Your personal data is collected solely to enable us to respond to your inquiry or provide you with relevant information about our services. We do not use this data for marketing purposes without your express consent.

3. Use of personal data

Your personal data may be used in the following ways:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • To inform you about changes to our services.
  • For administrative purposes such as data analysis, monitoring the quality of our services, etc.

4. Sharing personal data

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent, unless required by law.

5. Security of personal data

We protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss or damage using appropriate technical and organizational measures.

6. Your rights regarding personal data

You have the right to request access to your personal data, correct it, delete it or limit its processing. You can also withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal data at any time.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about our data protection policy or would like to exercise any of your rights relating to your personal data, please contact us at mkzaborowski@gmail.com.

Thank you for your trust and we assure you that your personal data is in safe hands with us.
